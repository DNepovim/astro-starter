## Admission

Free entry. Donations welcome. Come as you are.

## What to bring

Bring a cup if you want a drink, a bowl if you want food. No disposables on site — you've got everything you need at home anyway.

## Can I stay overnight?

Yes! Roll out a sleeping bag anywhere in the garden. The house has room too. Luggage storage available all day.

## Is it family-friendly?

Absolutely. Sandbox, climbing frame, and more for the little ones. And probably at least one friendly dog.
